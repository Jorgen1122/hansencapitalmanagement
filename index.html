About the Founder & Our Fund

Hansen Capital Management LLC was founded and is led by Jorgen Hansen. With inside industry experience, an education in finance and accounting, and a passion for the markets that began at age 16, Jorgen has spent years dedicated to researching the markets, and developing his edge in the market. The firm is built on disciplined, research-driven, and strategy-focused trading, with every decision grounded in quantitative principles. Leveraging proprietary methodologies, including the Hansen Setup, the firm identifies high-conviction opportunities while maintaining risk management and precision in execution. Years of testing these strategies, combined with a commitment to continous research analysis, ensure that our strategies are tested, refined, and implemented effectively. Hansen Capital Management is dedicated to delivering professional, strategy-driven fund management exclusively for accredited investors, combining discipline, innovation, and a data-driven approach to pursue high-growth opportunities.

Hansen Capital Management LLC is the General Partner of the Hansen Capital Fund, a Reg D private offering. Hansen Capital Management LLC is a subsidiary of Hansen Holdings LLC, the holding company for both Hansen Capital Management LLC and the Hansen Capital Fund. Hansen Holdings LLC also serves as a limited partner in the Hansen Capital Fund.
