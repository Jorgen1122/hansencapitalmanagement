About the Firm & Our Fund

Hansen Capital Management LLC, founded in 2025, manages the Hansen Capital Fund. We focus on quantitative methods for portfolio management. The firm leverages computer science, artificial intelligence, and advanced data analytics to develop and manage proprietary quantitative trading strategies, including the HECC model, designed to capture alpha in dynamic markets.

Hansen Capital Management LLC is a subsidiary of Hansen Holdings LLC and the general partner and manager of the Hansen Capital Fund, a Reg D private offering. Hansen Holdings LLC is the holdings company for its subsidiary Hansen Capital Management LLC and the Hansen Capital Fund. Hansen Holdings LLC also serves as a limited partner in the Hansen Capital Fund.
